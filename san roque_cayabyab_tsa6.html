Sung Jinwoo

Shadow Monarch

Sung Jinwoo (성진우, Seong Jin-Wu) is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling. He is the strongest hunter in the world and the second Shadow Monarch, having inherited the position from his predecessor Ashborn. Originally an infamously weak E-Rank Hunter, Jinwoo got the chance of a lifetime when he was selected as the Player of the System, giving him the unique ability to grow in strength without restraint. As a result, by taking advantage of the opportunities offered to him by the System, Jinwoo eventually rose to power as humanity's greatest hunter.

Cha Hae-In

S-Rank Hunter

Cha Hae-In (차해인) is a Korean S-Rank Hunter and the Vice-Guild Master of the Hunters Guild. She is also the wife of Sung Jinwoo and the mother of their son Sung Suho.

Baek Yoonho

Guild Master of the White Tiger Guild

Baek Yoonho (백윤호) is a Korean S-Rank Hunter who specializes in transformation magic and the Guild Master of the White Tiger Guild.

Ashborn

Monarch of Shadows

Ashborn (아스본) was the King of the Dead and the Monarch of Shadows. He was also the strongest Ruler and was known as the Greatest Fragment of Brilliant Light.

Antares

Monarch of Destruction

Antares (안타레스) was the King of Dragons and the Monarch of Destruction. He was also both the strongest and the oldest of the nine Monarchs, as well as the final antagonist of Solo Leveling.
